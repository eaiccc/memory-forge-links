Frequently Asked Questions

How does the spaced repetition system work?

MemoryForge uses the SM-2 algorithm to schedule card reviews. After each review, cards are graded (Again, Hard, Good, Easy) and the next review date is calculated based on your performance. Cards you find difficult appear more frequently.

Where is my data stored?

All flashcard data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to external servers. Your API keys (if used) are securely stored in the iOS Keychain.

How do I import cards?

Go to a deck and tap the import button. MemoryForge accepts JSON format with "front" and "back" fields for each card. An optional "notes" field is also supported.

How does AI card generation work?

MemoryForge can generate flashcards using AI providers (OpenAI, DeepSeek, Google Gemini). You provide a topic and the number of cards, and the AI creates question-answer pairs. An API key from the selected provider is required.

Is my data sent to AI providers?

Only the topic and generation parameters are sent when using AI card generation. Your existing flashcard content is never shared with any third party.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual cards or entire decks within the app. To remove all data, uninstall the app from your device.
