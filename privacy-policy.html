Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Introduction

MemoryForge ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our flashcard learning application.

Information We Collect

  • Flashcard Data: Cards and decks you create are stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in data storage.
  • API Keys: If you choose to use AI features, your API keys are securely stored in the iOS Keychain on your device.
  • Usage Statistics: We track AI generation usage locally to manage trial limits. This data never leaves your device.

How We Store Your Data

  • All your flashcard data is stored locally on your device.
  • API keys are encrypted and stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • We do not have servers that store your personal data.
  • We do not create accounts or require registration.

Third-Party Services

When you use AI card generation features, your prompts are sent to the AI provider you select:

  • OpenAI (GPT models)
  • DeepSeek
  • Google Gemini

Please review each provider's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data. We only send the topic and generation parameters — never your existing flashcard content.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Your flashcard content stays on your device.

Your Rights

  • Delete: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app.
  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app.
  • Control: You choose whether to use AI features and which provider to use.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app can be used without providing any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
